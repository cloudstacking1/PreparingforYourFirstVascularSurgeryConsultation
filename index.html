Vascular surgery is a medical specialty focusing on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of diseases affecting the vascular system—comprising arteries, veins, and lymphatic vessels. Conditions treated include peripheral artery disease (PAD), varicose veins, aneurysms, carotid artery disease, and deep vein thrombosis (DVT)..
